# Contributor Covenant Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Atlas respectful, practical, and free from harassment.

## Our Standards

Good behavior includes:

- focusing critique on code, docs, tests, product behavior, and release evidence
- being direct without being hostile
- redacting secrets, private keys, webhook URLs, customer data, private repo names, and full `.tracker` payloads
- giving maintainers enough detail to reproduce issues without dumping sensitive workspaces

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, personal attacks, or sustained disruption
- publishing private keys, tokens, webhook URLs, exploit payloads, or sensitive workspace archives in public issues
- spam, impersonation, or social-engineering attempts
- knowingly false or misleading security reports

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete unsafe content; close issues; block accounts; or move a report to a private channel when needed.

Report security-sensitive issues through [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). For conduct issues that are not security-sensitive, open a minimal public issue with no private details and ask for maintainer follow-up. A dedicated enforcement email is still an owner launch item.
