# Code Of Conduct

Be direct, respectful, and useful. Atlas is a technical project with security-sensitive workflows, so disagreement is expected; hostility is not.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus critique on code, docs, tests, and product behavior.
- Assume reports may involve sensitive local workspaces.
- Redact secrets, keys, customer data, private repo names, and full `.tracker` payloads.
- Give maintainers enough detail to reproduce a problem without dumping private material.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private keys, tokens, webhook URLs, or exploit details in public issues.
- Spam, impersonation, or social-engineering attempts.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete unsafe content; close issues; block accounts; or move a report to a private channel when needed. Security reports follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
