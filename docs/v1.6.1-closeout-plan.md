## Scope
v1.6.1 is the closeout and hardening train for v1.6.
It does not reopen product scope. It closes correctness, safety, auditability, and release-proof gaps in these areas:
1. deterministic migration convergence across independently upgraded v1.5 workspaces
2. strict sync-boundary enforcement for local-only artifacts
3. collaborator authority and lifecycle enforcement parity across surfaces
4. adversarial sync transport, stale conflict, and repair hardening
5. command/help/JSON/doctor closeout against the frozen v1.6 contract
6. hosted prerelease proof with durable evidence

## Contract additions
- Migration state is explicit per syncable legacy entity: `unstamped`, `stamped`, `divergent`, `unknown`.
- `stamped` never falls back to `unstamped`.
- `divergent` blocks sync apply until explicitly resolved.
- `sync status`, `inspect`, and `doctor` must agree on migration state.
- Read surfaces stay side-effect free, even after interrupted syncs or stale jobs.
- Local-only artifacts never publish, import, replay back, or repair back into existence.
- Embedded credentials are rejected or redacted everywhere.
- Hosted release claims require evidence from real published assets.
